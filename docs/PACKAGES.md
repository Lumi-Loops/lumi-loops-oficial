## Feature Breakdown

### Video Deliverables

- **Starter:** 4 short-form videos
- **Growth Engine:** 8 short-form videos
- **Marketing Partner:** 12+ custom videos with flexible types

### Video Length

- **Starter:** Up to 30 seconds (social media optimized)
- **Growth Engine:** Up to 45 seconds (extended social content)
- **Marketing Partner:** Custom lengths (shorts, explainers, long-form)

### Video Formats

- **Starter:** 1 format (choose Portrait OR Landscape)
- **Growth Engine:** 2 formats (both Portrait AND Landscape)
- **Marketing Partner:** All required formats

### Scripting

- **Starter:** Standard AI-generated scripts from your brief
- **Growth Engine:** Collaborative custom scriptwriting
- **Marketing Partner:** Full production team Script-to-Screen

### Voice-Over Quality

- **Starter:** Standard AI Voice
- **Growth Engine:** Premium AI Voice (Human-like quality)
- **Marketing Partner:** Premium AI Voice with full customization

### Revision Rounds

- **Starter:** 1 round of revisions per batch
- **Growth Engine:** 2 rounds of revisions per batch
- **Marketing Partner:** Unlimited revisions within scope

### Branding

- **Starter:** Standard Logo Watermark
- **Growth Engine:** Custom Branding (Colors, fonts, styling)
- **Marketing Partner:** Full Brand Integration across all assets

### Support Level

- **Starter:** Email Support
- **Growth Engine:** Priority Email & Chat Support
- **Marketing Partner:** Priority Support + Monthly Strategy Call

## Selection Guide

### Choose **AI-Video Starter** if you:

- Have a limited budget
- Need quick video content for a specific campaign
- Want to test AI video quality
- Don't need multiple format options

### Choose **Business Growth Engine** if you:

- Plan to use videos regularly
- Need both portrait and landscape formats
- Want professional collaboration on scripts
- Value custom branding elements
- Need faster response support

### Choose **AI Marketing Partner** if you:

- Need consistent, ongoing video production
- Want a dedicated strategy partner
- Require full-service production capabilities
- Prefer to outsource video marketing completely
- Benefit from monthly strategic consultations
